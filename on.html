HAIM - Women In Music Pt. III

"Women in Music Pt. III" is the third studio album by the band Haim,
released on June 26, 2020. The album consists of 16 songs with various genres
such as pop rock, R&B, and folk rock.
The album was produced by Ariel Rechtshaid, Rostam Batmanglij, and Danielle Haim.
The album received praise from music critics and was also included in
the best albums of 2020 list by several media.
