Haim is an American rock band consisting of three sisters: Este, Danielle, and Alana Haim.
They formed the band in 2007 and gained popularity with their debut album, "Days Are Gone," which was released in 2013.
The band's music incorporates elements of pop, rock, and R&B, and they have been
praised for their songwriting, musicianship, and vocal harmonies.
Some of their popular songs include "The Wire," "Want You Back," and "Summer Girl."
Haim has received several award nominations and has won awards such as the NME Award for Best International Band and the ASCAP Vanguard Award.
